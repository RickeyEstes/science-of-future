This presentation summarizes general concept of and basic results from the ongoing research project "Air-sea/land interaction: physics and observation of planetary boundary layers and quality of environment" supported by the Russian government mega-grant for the period 2011- 2014 and hosted by University of Nizhny Novgorod.
