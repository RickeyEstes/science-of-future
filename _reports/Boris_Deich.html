This article discusses the history of the development of non-formal education in Europe and Russia since the mid-nineteenth century to the present. In this article are defined three periods of development and some terms that denominate to non-formal education in these periods. It is also examined the modern approaches to the definition of non-formal education. The publication was prepared with the support of the Russian Foundation for Humanities Research (Research Project № 13-06-00162)
