--- layout: report page: author form: Poster speaker: Alexander Korneev position: PhD (candidate of science in physics and mathematics) title: Spin-off company and university-research. pros and cons section: Physics, Nanotechnologies, Materials Technology, Space organization: Moscow State Pedagogical University authors: [ Alexander Korneev ] ---

The talk will present the route of innovation from the demonstration of the physical effect to the commercial product commercialized through a small innovation company. The benefits and problems of the close integration of the scientific laboratory at the University and a spin-off company will be demonstrated by the success story of Scontel company and radiophyscs laboratory at Moscow State Pedagogical University.
